The Opportunity Cost of
What You Gave Up By
Mowing the Grass:
Seeing the movie with your friends.

Earning $20 was worth more to you than seeing the movie. Plus you saved $10 by not buying a movie ticket.

Going to the Movie:
$10 for movie tickets + $20 for mowing grass = $30

You wanted to see the movie with your friends more than you wanted what you could buy with $30.
